Welcome!

I am a third year graduate student of astronomy and astrobiology at the
University of Washington in Seattle. I study extrasolar planets (exoplanets),
that is, planets orbiting stars other than The Sun. Exoplanets are faint
and distant, presenting an exciting challenge for even the most sophisticated
modern astronomical techniques.
